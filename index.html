We've just launched!

Research Software Engineers (RSEs) are people who develop software and/or support the use of computers and computer technologies for research purposes. They can work in a variety of job roles.

The RSE Leeds network launched in December 2016. It aims to connect staff at the University of Leeds who develop and support research software.

What's the plan?

The 50 researchers and support staff who attended the kick-off meeting had a lot of great ideas. Based on their input we're developing a programme of meetings and presentations.
